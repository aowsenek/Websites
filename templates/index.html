Digital Time Capsule

I am conducting research on how to create a Digital Time Capsule (DTC) that is completely self-contained with 7-15 year power. This DTC will be able to store and retrieve large amounts of data, connect wirelessly, remain dormant for 5-15 years at a time, at which time it will self-activate and start broadcasting to the world.

It is the end of things
Of Suffering, Pain, of Sadness
And while we contemplate in quiet fear
We realize it is also the end of good:
Happiness, Joy, Elation?
All gone.
Because it is the end of all things.

We will not live to see the future
As nature reclaims what once was wild
As she blossoms forth again and again
Time wearing all things to a fine dust.
But even she, too, shall perish
And one day
What was left of our existence
Will be gone.
For it is the end of all things.
Of our lives, our existence, and our world.

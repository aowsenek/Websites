About Me

I'm a student at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. My senior research lab is the Microelectronics Lab under director David Bell. I have taken an assortment of electronics and programming classes, and run the main TJ Electronics page(tjelectronics.tk). My Digital Time Capsule was initially an idea I thought of when pondering all the funny, interesting, cool stories people have from their time at TJ, and I wanted a way to preserve that and pass it down to a later generation of students to reflect upon.

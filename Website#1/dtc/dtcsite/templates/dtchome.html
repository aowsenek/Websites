Digital Time Capsule

This is a Time Capsule that stores digital information such as photos and video. It automatically starts up years from its hibernation date, bearing said memories for people (like you) to connect to the ad-hoc network and web server it autonomously starts. It (the device) is isolated and requires no external power, influence, or other meddling for it to operate successfully.

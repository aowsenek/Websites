Digital Time Capsule

I am developing a Digital Time Capsule that will be able to store digital information such as photos and video. It will automatically start up years in the future bearing said memories for whomever connects to the ad-hoc network and web server it autonomously starts. I am using a revolutionary computing technology to make this device affordable and low power. I intend for the device to be isolated and require no external power, influence, or other meddling for it to operate successfully.
